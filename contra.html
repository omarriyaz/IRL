CONTRA

Contra is an application that allow's users all around the world to interact with each other and share their memories and experiences through scrapbooks


What exciting features does contra provide?
- Contra allows users to create and post scrapbooks
- Contra has a feed where users can view other scrapbooks
- Contra allows users to view other scrapbooks nearby through Augmented Reality
- Contra allows users to create and join likeminded communities
